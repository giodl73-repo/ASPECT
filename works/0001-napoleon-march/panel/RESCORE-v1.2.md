## Data/Story Integrity — Sub-step Assessment

**Step A — Source quality declaration:**  
Troop counts assembled from fragmentary military records; Minard himself acknowledges they are estimates (stated in his own panel review). The counts are presented as precise figures on the band. Precision implied > precision available. **Confidence suppression: dock −2 (knowingly; estimates presented as measurements).**

**Step B — Accuracy tradeoff:**  
No accuracy tradeoff. The flow band is the most appropriate encoding for this data type — there is no more accurate alternative Minard was forgoing. **No adjustment.**

**Step C — Causation claim integrity:**  
Temperature juxtaposition implies cold → soldier deaths. The relationship is real and significant but is not the only cause (disease, starvation, and combat also contributed). The implied causal primacy of cold is plausible but overstated. **Dock −1.5 (implied causation; plausible, moderate overstatement).**

**Total sub-step dock: −3.5**
