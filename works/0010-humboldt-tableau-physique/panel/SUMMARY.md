## Key Agreements

**Legibility is the weakest dimension -- all scores 11-12.** This is the strongest panel consensus in any dimension across all ten works. All five reviewers independently arrive at 11 or 12 for Legibility. The reason is consistent: the plate requires the accompanying essay to supply the analytical framework; without the essay, the viewer sees data but does not grasp the argument. The meteorological columns require careful cross-referencing with the mountain profile. The primary reading context (contemplative study, scientific audience) helps, but even a specialist reader requires extended effort to synthesize nine variables.

**The intrinsic-complexity consensus (Cluster H) is unanimous.** All five reviewers accept that the nine-variable complexity is systemic -- it represents one multi-dimensional ecological system, not nine independent measurements. No reviewer docks Visual Economy for the number of variables (only for encoding-type inefficiencies). This is the Cluster H confirmation needed for a rubric amendment.

**Data/Story Integrity clusters at 14-15.** The framework-in-essay vs. framework-on-plate split is identified by all reviewers as the primary integrity concern. All dock for Step D; all dock for Step A (undeclared measurement precision). The scoring is consistent.

---

## Key Tensions

**Type B vs. Type C classification:**

The brief notes this may be Type B or Type C. The panel's consensus is Type B (Founder): ecological visualization as a school did not exist before this plate; Humboldt's multi-variable cross-section encoding is a genuine invention; the founding grammar is recoverable (with essay support). The Type C classification would apply only if Humboldt was deliberately synthesizing two established schools (scientific illustration + cartography), and there is no evidence of that deliberate synthesis -- the plate is a new form, not a combination of known forms.

Verdict: Type B. Score accordingly.

**How severely does Step D dock for the framework-in-essay situation?**

Minard and Tufte dock -2 for the framework-in-essay split. Bertin docks -2 for the same. The Artist docks -1 (arguing the essay is immediately adjacent and accessible). The Historian docks -2. Range: -1 to -2. Consensus at -2 is correct: the framework is declared (in the essay) but not on the plate itself, and the plate is sometimes encountered without the essay (reproduced independently). The dock should be intermediate between undeclared (-3) and in-visualization declared (0). The rubric's Step D wording should specify this gradation.

**Humboldt's scientific importance vs. his visualization quality:**

The Historian notes that biogeography is more consequential than statistical graphics, but the rubric does not score scientific importance -- it scores visualization quality. This is a productive clarification: the rubric is correctly not scoring the significance of the underlying discovery. The Tableau Physique is a good but not great visualization; its historical importance is overwhelming. These are different things.

## Cluster H -- Intrinsic Complexity: CONFIRMED across 0006 + 0010

**Works: 0006 (Haeckel) + 0010 (Humboldt). Reviewers: multiple in both works.**

Haeckel innovations: #53 (compound iconic encoding), #55 (intrinsic complexity distinction), #57 (accuracy-generated beauty). Humboldt innovations: Minard #91 (systemic data), Artist #100 (Cluster H confirmation), plus unanimous panel agreement that nine variables are intrinsically required.

The cluster is confirmed. The amendment: add to the Visual Economy dimension a preliminary assessment question -- "Is the visual complexity intrinsic to the subject (required by the data's systemic structure) or added by the designer?" Intrinsic complexity is not chartjunk and should not be scored as visual excess. The Visual Economy score should reflect excess above the intrinsic requirement, not total complexity.

**Proposed v1.5 amendment (to accompany Cluster G amendment):**

> Visual Economy assessment: before applying anchors, assess whether visual complexity is (a) intrinsic -- the subject matter is genuinely multi-variable, multi-dimensional, or structurally complex, and the encoding represents this accurately, or (b) additive -- complexity added by the designer beyond what the data requires. Score on excess above intrinsic necessity. Intrinsic complexity at high fidelity should score in the 12-14 range regardless of total mark count.

### Cluster H -- Intrinsic Complexity: AMENDMENT THRESHOLD MET (0006 + 0010)
Amendment proposal ready. Add intrinsic-complexity preliminary assessment to Visual Economy: before scoring, determine whether complexity is intrinsic (systemic data requirement) or additive (designer-added). Score on excess above intrinsic necessity.

### Cluster J -- Explicit Declaration as Founding Virtue (0009): CONFIRMED
Amendment proposal ready. Amend School Fidelity Type B anchors to distinguish: (a) grammar declared in the work or immediate context (higher score), (b) grammar derivable from the work but not declared (current standard), (c) grammar requiring expert analysis to reconstruct (lower score).

### Step D Gradation -- Framework Declaration Levels: NEW AMENDMENT TRIGGER (0010)
Innovations 92 and 96 (Minard and Tufte) both propose that Step D should specify gradations of framework declaration: within the visualization (dock 0-1), in adjacent essay/documentation (dock 1-2), not declared but inferable (dock 2-3), not declared and non-obvious (dock 3). This refines the existing Step D anchors rather than replacing them.

### E2 Taxonomy: STRUCTURAL REVISION RECOMMENDED
After ten works, five E2 sub-type candidates (E2a epistemic, E2b political-testimonial, E2c scholarly canonization, E2d productive-appropriation, and candidates for E2e diaspora-institutional and E2f disciplinary-founding). The sub-type taxonomy is not scaling well. Recommendation: replace content-based taxonomy with mechanism-based taxonomy before ratifying any E2 amendments. Two mechanism dimensions: (1) speed of recognition (contemporaneous / delayed / very delayed) x (2) agency of canonization (self-evident / scholarly constructed / politically constructed / institutionally transmitted). This 2x3 matrix would accommodate all observed E2 cases without proliferating sub-types.
