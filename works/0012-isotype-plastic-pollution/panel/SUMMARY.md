## Key Agreements

**The annotation panel is the single biggest design failure.** Neurath says it substitutes text for a second ISOTYPE chart. Tufte says it is illegible at 2m. Nightingale says the advocacy-critical context cannot reach the glancing visitor. The Statistician calls it "present but inaccessible." The Audience lens confirms: the text cannot be read at display scale. Five reviewers, five angles, one finding.

**The repetition grammar works correctly.** The partial symbol extension is consistent with historical ISOTYPE practice. Symbol-counting behavior (Innovation #114) is a genuine positive finding.

**The absolute-only framing is a real integrity problem.** Data/Story Integrity scores 14-15 across the panel, reflecting a consistent view that absolute ocean plastic inputs without per-capita context is a Step B accuracy tradeoff that materially misleads the intended audience.

---

## Key Tensions

**Neurath vs. Tufte on the right remedy:**

Neurath prescribes a second per-capita ISOTYPE chart — the school-faithful, advocacy-complete solution. Tufte prescribes removing the annotation panel and changing the title to accurately describe what the chart shows — accepting the limitation, making it visible through framing. These are incompatible. The designer must choose: correct the advocacy failure (wrong conclusion reaches visitors) or the grammar failure (text substitutes for pictographic comparison).

**Nightingale on advocacy form:**

Nightingale raises whether ISOTYPE is the right school for this story — a form like a coxcomb might embed the normalization in the visual structure rather than annotation. This echoes Work 0011 Innovation #103 (school-story mismatch). Open question for revision.

---

## Design Revision Recommendations

1. **Create a paired per-capita chart** — second ISOTYPE chart on same plate showing regions by per-capita plastic production; let the two-chart comparison carry the moral argument the annotation panel fails to deliver.
2. **Fix annotation panel text size** — if paired chart cannot be produced, minimum 14pt text in annotation zone; redesign as large-typography visual zone, not text block.
3. **Declare uncertainty** — model-estimated figures (±30-40% uncertainty in some regions) warrant a sentence acknowledging estimation, not direct measurement.
4. **Consider limiting subtitle** — "Total tonnes entering the ocean" immediately constrains viewer conclusion even at 2m.
5. **Test symbol legibility with real audiences** — bespoke ocean-plastic symbol has no accumulated recognition.
