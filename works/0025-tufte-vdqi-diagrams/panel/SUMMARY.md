## Key Agreements

**The School Fidelity question has a circular but valid answer.** All five reviewers address the central problem: what does School Fidelity mean when the school IS the subject matter? The answer is that the self-exemplifying property (the work is an instance of its own principles) makes the school-fidelity test "does the work practice what it preaches?" The answer is yes, and this is both a genuine finding and logically required by the work's founding character.

**The meta-encoding grammar is consistent and learnable.** Bertin (Innovation #199) names the meta-visualization grammar's visual variables: annotation arrows, callout text, before/after sequences, removal highlighting. All reviewers recognize these as a consistent, learnable apparatus. The grammar is the founding act of the information-architecture school.

**The lie factor is the consistent integrity gap.** All five reviewers independently flag the lie factor examples as having Step A undeclared measurement choices. The principle is correct; the specific calculations are slightly overconfident. Consistent dock of approximately −2 to −3 across all reviewers on Data/Story Integrity.

**Craft 15/15 is the project's first perfect consensus score.** No reviewer finds any craft gap. Tufte's investment in the printing quality and typographic precision produces a work that is its own best argument.

---

## Key Tensions

**Bertin on theory vs. principles:**
Bertin notes that his own *Sémiologie Graphique* provides the theoretical foundation (systematic grammar of visual variables) that Tufte's principled prescriptions lack. Tufte's approach is more persuasive; Bertin's is more generalizable. The tension is productive: the field needs both systematic grammar and practical persuasion. These are complementary rather than competing.

**Playfair on the limits of Tufte's principles:**
Playfair argues that Tufte takes some principles further than the evidence supports (lie factor calculations) and that some of Tufte's prescriptions are more appropriate for expert audiences than for general audiences. This echoes the tension in the broader project between Tufte's data-ink-ratio standard and Nightingale's/Du Bois's accessibility-first approach.

---

## The Circular Review Problem and Its Resolution

The substitution of Playfair for Tufte is the correct move, and the panel found it productive. Playfair is the practitioner Tufte theorizes; he has historical standing to evaluate whether the theory matches the practice. His review (89/100) is the highest individual score in the panel, indicating that the founding practitioner endorses the founding theorist's account of the practice.

The Inverse Tufte Problem (named here after Innovation #174): Tufte's principles have shaped the panel's critical framework across 24 works. His absence from this review does not mean his influence is absent — every reviewer has internalized elements of Tufte's approach. The substitution prevents direct circularity while acknowledging that the indirect circularity is unavoidable.

## What This Work Reveals

**The rubric works for meta-visualization — with one modification.** The School Fidelity dimension handles the circular problem through the self-exemplifying criterion: the work is assessed against its own principles. This is a degenerate case of Type B (school founder) assessment that the rubric did not anticipate but handles correctly under the existing logic.

**A new school is formally confirmed.** Works 0025 founds the information-architecture school within the DEGAS project. The school has: a founding grammar (Innovation #201), a meta-encoding visual variable set (Innovation #199), a distinctive resonance mode (Innovation #200), and a documented body of subsequent practitioners. The school is now formally documented alongside the 11 others in schools/.

**The highest score in the project (88.5) belongs to a work about visualization, not a visualization.** This is not coincidental: a work that selects and curates the best examples of a practice, demonstrates principles through those examples, and is designed to the highest craft standard has significant systematic advantages over a work that must represent raw data honestly. The meta-visualization advantage is real and should be noted when comparing scores across schools.
