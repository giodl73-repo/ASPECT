# Nightingale — 0032 Global Smartphone Supply Chain

## School Fidelity /20 → 16

The advocacy dimension here is implicit rather than explicit: the supply chain vulnerability is shown, not argued. The diagram reveals that the global smartphone supply chain has dangerous concentration at the middle stages — but it does not tell the viewer what to do about it. For an advocacy visualization, this would be a fidelity failure. For a schematic cartography work, it is appropriate — the school's grammar shows topology; the advocacy argument is available from the topology if the viewer brings the question.

I assess this as a schematic cartography work that has an implicit advocacy argument: the convergence pattern and red chokepoint markers carry a clear implication without stating it. This is effective because policy audiences are often more persuaded by data that lets them draw their own conclusions than by data that tells them what to conclude.

## Data/Story Integrity /20 → 16

Accurate topology, well-sourced. The chokepoint threshold declaration is needed (per Beck and Tufte). The date stamp is important given the rapidly changing lithium processing landscape.

## Legibility /15 → 14

The stage column structure makes the diagram readable for a policy audience. The convergence pattern at the center-left of the diagram (Processing and Components columns) is the argument's visual center of gravity, and it is immediately visible. The chokepoint markers (red fill) direct attention without requiring text explanation.

## Visual Economy /15 → 14

Economical for the subject matter. No unnecessary elements.

## Resonance /15 → 13

The convergence gestalt produces the correct resonance for a policy audience: a sense of "I see the vulnerability — I see where it is and how severe it is." This is more muted than direct advocacy visualization, but appropriate for a schematic cartography work. Type IV fires strongly; Type III fires partially.

## Craft /15 → 14

Well-designed and precise.

## My Total: 87/100

A strong supply chain schematic with implicit advocacy resonance. The stage-anchor solution is the work's most important innovation.
