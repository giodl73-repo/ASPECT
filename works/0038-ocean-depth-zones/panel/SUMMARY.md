## CLUSTER U - CONFIRMED ACROSS WORKS 0031 + 0038

**Cluster U: Axis inversion as ecological grammar requirement** confirmed across two works.

Work 0031 (#221 Minard, #224 Historian): downward axis first confirmed for soil depth visualization. Work 0038 (#253 Minard, #254 Bertin, #257 Historian): confirmed for ocean depth visualization. Different medium, dramatically different scale (11,000m vs. 150cm), same gravitational logic.

**The confirmed principle -- Gravitational Hierarchy in Ecological Visualization:** the primary axis should follow the direction of the physical gradient under gravity. Altitude ecology: axis upward. Below-surface ecology: axis downward.

Three ecological contexts confirmed: (1) mountain altitude -- Humboldt 1807, axis upward; (2) soil depth -- work 0031, axis downward; (3) ocean depth -- this work, axis downward.

**Amendment recommendation:** Add to School Fidelity dimension, ecological-visualization school: gravitational hierarchy principle -- the primary axis should follow the direction of the physical gradient under gravity. Diagrams that invert the gravitational direction without ecological justification dock School Fidelity 1-2 points.
