## The Advocacy-as-Coercion vs. Advocacy-as-Persuasion Distinction

**Advocacy-as-persuasion:** presents evidence, makes an argument, lets the audience decide. The viewer can ask "is this true?" and find an answer in the work.

**Advocacy-as-coercion:** targets psychological vulnerabilities to produce action before rational assessment. This poster engineers guilt before the viewer can ask whether guilt is warranted. Key diagnostic: no data, no checkable claims, mechanism designed to bypass interrogation.
