# Nightingale Review — 0049 WWI British Recruitment Propaganda

I designed visualizations to reach people who would not otherwise see the data. I chose forms that would persuade decision-makers. I understand the challenge of making data move an audience.

But I always had data. I had the tables from Scutari. I had the mortality numbers. I had the before-and-after comparison. My polar area diagrams were advocacy, yes — but they were advocacy built on real numbers that the viewer could check. If someone wanted to dispute my coxcomb, they could ask for the underlying tables. I provided them.

This poster has no underlying table. There is no data to check. The argument is entirely emotional: feel guilty, enlist. The guilt is engineered, not evidenced.

I want to be precise about what the distinction is. Advocacy visualization presents evidence in a form designed to produce action. The evidence must be real; the action must follow from the evidence honestly. I showed that preventable deaths exceeded battlefield deaths; the action (sanitary reform) followed logically from that evidence. The viewer could accept or reject the argument on its merits.

This poster presents no evidence. It presents a scene designed to trigger a psychological response (guilt) that precedes any rational assessment. The viewer is supposed to feel guilty before they think about whether the guilt is warranted. This is not advocacy; it is emotional engineering.

The craft is competent. Savile Lumley was a skilled illustrator. The composition is clear; the figures are readable; the typography serves the message. I do not dispute the technical execution.

But good craft in service of manipulation is not a virtue. The doctors at Scutari had good craft. Their administration was also killing soldiers through poor sanitation. Good process does not justify bad outcomes.

I will score the Integrity dimension very low because the integrity failure is structural. A work that makes a claim without evidence, and is designed to prevent the viewer from asking whether the claim is true, has failed at Integrity in the most fundamental way.
