## The Self-Referential Note

Work 0050 appears as a bar in the chart it depicts. This is a declared recursion: the chart includes its own score (90.6) as the bar labeled "0050-degas-scoreboard." The chart cannot be complete without this bar; the score cannot be assigned without reviewing the chart; the chart cannot be finalized until the score is assigned. This is a mild bootstrapping problem that the design resolves by treating 0050 as any other work -- reviewed, scored, placed in the chart.

The Cluster S domain resonance (+1) applies: a DEGAS visualization OF DEGAS visualizations is exactly the self-referential grammar that Cluster S identifies.
