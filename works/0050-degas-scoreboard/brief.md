# Brief: DEGAS Scoreboard as Statistical-Graphics

## Subject

The scores of all 50 DEGAS works (0001-0050), displayed as a sorted horizontal bar chart. Each bar = one work; bar length = v2.2 score; bars sorted descending; color = school; labels = work slug. The meta-visualization: DEGAS documenting its own scores.

## Design Intent

A proper Playfair bar chart, not data-art. The design should not be inflated by its self-referential subject. Score it as a statistical-graphics work like any other. The only special property: domain-gated resonance — the designer who worked through all 50 works sees the project history in the sorted bars.

## School
